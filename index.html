Steve Jobs

The man who founded Apple.

Portrait of Steve Jobs.


Timeline of Steve Jobs life:


  • 1955: Born in San Francisco, California.

  • 1968 - 1972: Attends at Homestead High School.

  • 1974: Gets a job on Atari for some months.

  • 1976: Apple I is released by Steve Jobs & Steve Wozinak.

  • 1977: Apple II is released.

  • 1984: Macintosh is released.

  • 1986: Steve Jobs buys Pixar.

  • 1997: Jobs returns to Apple.

  • 2001: iPod is released.

  • 2003: A tumor is discovered on Steve's body.

  • 2007: iPhone and iPad are released.

  • 2009: Netbook is released.

  • 2011: iCloud services become avaliable.
    Steve Jobs dies at the age of 56.
